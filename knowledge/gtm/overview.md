## Strategic Priorities

1. **AO Agent Orchestrator** — highest differentiation, unique in market. Hero content: "180 PRs in 7 days." HN/Product Hunt launch will drive the most awareness.
2. **LaunchApp Templates** — commercial revenue opportunity. The "AI-workforce-maintained" angle is a strong differentiator vs ShipFast/Makerkit. Recommend $149–$249 one-time pricing.
3. **Better Auth** — ecosystem play. launchapp-dev benefits from Better Auth adoption by producing the best integration guides and comparison content.
4. **LaunchPad BaaS** — reposition as the SDK layer beneath templates rather than standalone BaaS. Needs dev investment before a standalone relaunch.

## Cross-Product Themes

- **"AI workforce"** — AO powers the template's own development; lean into this story
- **"Zero vendor lock-in"** — self-hosted auth (Better Auth), own your database (PostgreSQL), own your files (S3)
- **"Modern TypeScript stack"** — React Router 7, Hono, Drizzle, Better Auth, Biome — not another Next.js template
- **"Ships daily"** — AO agents update the template 24/7; freshness is a product feature
