# LaunchApp Vision

## Mission

**Make it easy to launch apps. Rocket-launch businesses through software enablement.**

## What This Means

LaunchApp exists to remove every barrier between "I have an idea" and "I have a running business." We build the tools, templates, infrastructure, and AI workforce that lets founders ship production software in days, not months.

## How We Do It

### The Stack

1. **LaunchApp Templates** — Production-ready SaaS starters. Pick a template, customize, deploy. You have a business.
2. **LaunchPad BaaS** — The backend primitives (auth, db, storage, realtime, payments) that power every template. Swap in, swap out, own your data.
3. **AO Agent Orchestrator** — The AI workforce that builds, tests, reviews, and ships code autonomously. 180+ PRs in 7 days. Your team never sleeps.
4. **Better Auth** — Authentication that you own. No vendor lock-in. Self-hosted. Works everywhere.
5. **Design System** — Beautiful, accessible UI components. Radix + Tailwind. Drop in and ship.

### The Philosophy

- **Zero vendor lock-in** — self-hosted auth, own your database, own your files
- **Modern TypeScript** — React Router 7, Hono, Drizzle, Biome — not another Next.js clone
- **AI-native** — AO agents maintain the templates 24/7, so they're always up to date
- **Ships daily** — freshness is a product feature, not a nice-to-have

## Product Evolution

### Phase 1: Foundation (Current)
- Build the core templates and SDKs
- Prove AO can autonomously maintain a production codebase
- Validate demand (Reddit response confirmed interest in AO)

### Phase 2: Revenue
- Launch template sales (the product exists, needs checkout)
- AO Pro/Team for developers who want orchestration
- Open-source what should be open, monetize what should be paid

### Phase 3: Platform
- LaunchPad becomes the SDK layer that all templates share
- AO Cloud for teams who don't want to self-host
- Marketplace for templates, plugins, and components

### Phase 4: Ecosystem
- Third-party templates built on LaunchPad
- Third-party AO plugins and agent personas
- LaunchApp becomes the platform others build on

## The Portfolio

Every repo in launchapp-dev serves the mission. Some are:
- **Core products** — templates, SDKs, AO, auth, design system
- **Supporting tools** — worktree-manager, openapi-gen, plugin packs
- **Early client work** — projects from LaunchApp's first engagements
- **Experiments** — explorations that may or may not become products

Not everything ships. Not everything survives. But everything exists because someone was trying to make it easier to launch an app.
