# launchpad-appstores

> Server-side Apple App Store Connect and Google Play receipt validation and subscription lifecycle management.

## Purpose

Handles in-app purchase validation, subscription lifecycle events, and webhook processing for both Apple App Store Connect and Google Play. Provides mock providers for testing environments without requiring live store credentials.

## Visibility: Private
## Maturity: Single initial commit. Complete but not yet iterated on.

## Tech Stack

- TypeScript
- Node.js
- Mock providers for testing

## Package

`@launchpad/appstores` (v0.1.0)

## Dependencies

- Part of the LaunchPad BaaS platform

## Status

142 tests, 100% coverage. Single initial commit — feature-complete for its scope but no subsequent iterations.
