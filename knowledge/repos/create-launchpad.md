## Purpose

CLI scaffolding tool to bootstrap a new Launchpad BaaS project. The Launchpad-specific counterpart to `create-launchapp` — targets teams adopting the full `@launchpad/*` BaaS platform rather than the standalone `launchapp-lite` template.

## Dependencies on Org Products

- Bootstraps projects that use `launchpad-baas` and its SDKs
- Companion to `create-launchapp`
